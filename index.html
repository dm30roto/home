About

My name is Dan Rotondo. I'm a UI developer and designer. I have a M.S. in Information Technology from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a concentration in Human-Computer Interaction and a B.S in Computer Science from Siena College with at minor in Multimedia Studies.

My philosophy in design and development is make things clean and simple. My passion is taking complex workflows and interactions and making a simple and consistent user experience from them. I'm interested in most things involving the web, especially mobile web applications. I'm always looking for the latest and greatest innovations throughout the tech community as sources of inspiration.

I love what I do for work and I love what I do outside of work. Some of my favorite hobbies are hiking, cooking, photography, attending sporting events, and traveling.
